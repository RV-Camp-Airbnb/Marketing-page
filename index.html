About Us

5th wheel Airbnb is a connection between land owners and 5th wheel / RV owners. RV parks are often cramped and in many areas are booked months in advance.

Collectively, landowners hold vast swaths of unused land that could be earning them revenue. By using 5th wheel Airbnb, RV Owners get access to use these previously unknown/unavailable sites, and Landowners get to cash in on otherwise dormant or underutilized land.

So whether you're an avid explorer travelling across the country, or a home owner with only enough property for a single RV, 5th Wheel Airbnb is for you.
